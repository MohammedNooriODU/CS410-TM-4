# The Problem
When traveling or moving into a new location, finding new restaurants can be a daunting and indecisive task, especially in an unfamiliar location. Traditional restaurant recommendation apps present too many choices without the ability to customize options to the user’s preference, making them feel overwhelmed. 
Furthermore, the popularity of fast-food and well-known restaurants can easily draw attention away from “mom and pop” and “hole in the wall" restaurants. 
Finding a place to eat has the potential to take and waste a lot of time searching through lists of restaurants and the possibility of missing out on a unique food experience.
